  • Count Sort

Linear sorting algorithms are simple sorting algorithms that operate in a linear time complexity, typically O(n) in the best cases, where "n" is the number of elements to be sorted. These algorithms are often less efficient than more advanced sorting algorithms like quicksort, mergesort, or heapsort, but they are easy to understand and implement.
